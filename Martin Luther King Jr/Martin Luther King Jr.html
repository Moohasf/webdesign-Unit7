Martin Luther King Jr. was a public speaker that helped to get equal rights to black people in America. His most famous speech, I Have A Dream, was also his last. While speaking, he was assassinated.

He was born on January 15th, 1929, in Atlanta, Georgi. Throughout his life, he wasan American Baptist, activist, humanitarian, and a key member of the African-American civil rights movement.
